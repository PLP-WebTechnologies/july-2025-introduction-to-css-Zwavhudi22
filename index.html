Mostly Granny Squares

A Granny Square is a crocheted square made by stitching in rounds from center outward. These squares are always handmade, as machines can't replicate the crochet technique, and they often look like thick lace. While it's possible to make one large granny square, crafters typically create many small ones called motifs which they then join together to form items like clothes, bags, blankets, and other home accesories.
